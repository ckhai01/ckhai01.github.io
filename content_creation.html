Content creation


What is content creation?


Simply put, Content creation is the production of digital media for viewing by digital audiences. In the context of cyber gaming, we are talking about mainly 2 aspects: streamers and youtubers. In this article, we will be talking about how gaming has influenced both aspects.


Why these two aspects?

Many of us here have experienced some sort of a lockdown or quarantine due to the presence of COVID-19 virus. being confined at home or a facility isn't great. With the restrictions in place, the number of activities we can do is very little. To help pass the time, we turn to social media: instagram, youtube, the likes. Youtube views soared, streamers became more active because that's all they can do: stream content. Gaming hours rose, and with it, the interest in gaming videos.
