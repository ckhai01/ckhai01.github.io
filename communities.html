Gaming Communities


3 Gaming Comunities for You to Consider Joining

Reddit

Redit is basically a repository for all types of gamers no matter the genre. There are all sorts of things waiting to be discovered, ranging from the lore to easter eggs to walkthrough gameplays. You can also go to discussions and interact with the other users on the forum. There are general subreddits like r/gaming or subreddits for specific games like r/Genshin_Impact, r/csgo and r/CallOfDuty.

Steam

While Steam is commonly known to be a distributer of games, they too have their own communities and forums for games. This site is straightforward and has a wide range of sections such as; game guides, reviews, gamer artwork, live play broadcasts, in-play videos from users, screenshots of in-game moments and even workshop items from novice to pro designers. Players can also trade game items for real life cash. For example, selling of loot crates in the shop for games like CSGO.

Discord

Discord is basically a Skype for gamers. It allows users to message each other, get into calls for easier communication and form group chats. Furthermore, similar to game forums, one can join groups created for game discussions etc. They would then be able to make friends with people of the same interests and enjoy the game together. Discord is also competitable with all opperating systems (e.g. Windows, MacOS, Linux...).
